How to Use Beauty Queen

Follow these simple steps to get started

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email address. You'll receive an OTP for verification. Once verified, you can start shopping!

2

Browse Products

Explore our wide range of beauty products. Use filters to find products by category or search for specific items.

3

Add to Cart

Click "Add to Cart" on any product you like. You can adjust quantities in your cart before checkout.

4

Checkout & Payment

Review your cart and proceed to checkout. Choose between Cash on Delivery or Online Payment via Razorpay.

5

Track Your Order

After placing your order, you can track it from your dashboard. We'll keep you updated on the delivery status.

Happy Shopping!
